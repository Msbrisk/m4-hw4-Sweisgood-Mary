BONO

Musician, Activist, Philanthropist

While Paul Hewson, aka Bono, is most well known for his music career, his work as an activist and philanthropist are legendary.

Lead Singer and Founding member of the legendary rock band, U2.

Bono answered an ad for musicians to form a band that he saw on a flier at his high school.

The group has now been together for 48 years and has 15 studio albums.

Activism and Philanthropy

Bono does not hesitate to use his celebrity for the greater good.

He is very involved with Amnesty International and his charity We Aree Family Foundation that combats AIDS and hunger in Africa.
